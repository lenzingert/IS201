My Projects

    1. How to Wash a Car simple steps
      • Collect the supplies
      • Rinse car then proceed to wash with proper detailing soap
      • Rinse and dry vehice with blower and proper microfiber towels
    2. How to protect a vehicles exterior simple steps
      • Once cleaned and dry grab clay and clay lubricant and follow instructions on the bottle
      • Next grab your favorite wax / sealant and microfiber applicator and follow the bottle instructions
      • Take a fluffy microfiber and buff off the product and admire your work
    3. How to detail the interior of a vehicle simple steps
      • Remove all trash and vaccum the interior
      • Grab an all purpose cleaner and a brush and clean everthing
      • Protect the interior with a water based dressing for shine
